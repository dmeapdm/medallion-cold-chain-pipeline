# Sample data guidance

Only upload sanitized demonstration data to GitHub.

## Files expected in this folder

* `telemetry_sample.json`
* `sensores_limites_sample.csv`

## Recommended content

Keep only a few rows that are enough to demonstrate:

* valid readings
* at least one low-temperature alert
* at least one high-temperature alert
* one example of an invalid or rejected reading if you want to illustrate Silver quality rules

## Safety rules

* do not upload production data
* do not upload secrets or credentials
* do not upload internal identifiers if they are sensitive
* keep the sample small and easy to read
